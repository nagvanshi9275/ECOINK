## 1. The SEO Dashboard (Your Secret Weapon)
On every Project, Blog, and Service page, you will now see an **SEO Meta Box** at the bottom. This is where you control how Google sees your page.

### Key Tools:
*   **Focus Keyphrase:** Tell the system which "Search Word" you want to rank for (e.g., *"Custom Kitchens Melbourne"*).
*   **SEO Title:** This is the blue link people click on Google.
*   **Meta Description:** The small paragraph under the link on Google that "sells" your page to the user.
*   **Social Preview:** See exactly how your page will look when shared on **Facebook or Twitter**.

## 2. Real-Time SEO Scoring (0-100)
As you type, our "Smart Engine" calculates an SEO score for you. It‚Äôs like a personal SEO consultant sitting next to you!

*   **Green (Good):** You're doing great! Google will love this.
*   **Orange (Warning):** Small fixes needed (e.g., "Your title is a bit too short").
*   **Red (Error):** Critical issue (e.g., "You forgot to add a Meta Description").

**What makes a 100/100 score?**
1.  Using your **Keyphrase** in the title and the first paragraph.
2.  Keeping titles between **50-60 characters** (so they don't get cut off).
3.  Writing a description that is helpful and inviting.

## 3. Readability & Content Analysis
Google prioritizes pages that are easy for humans to read. Our system checks:
*   **Reading Ease:** Are your sentences too long or complicated?
*   **Word Count:** Is there enough information for Google to "understand" the page?
*   **Syllacle Count:** It automatically suggests if your vocabulary is too complex for a general audience.

## 4. Image SEO (Faster & Smarter)
Images are a huge part of your business. We've made them "Google-friendly" automatically:

*   **Alt Text:** You can now add descriptions to every image (e.g., *"Modern Black Kitchen Wardrobe"*). Google uses this text to "see" your images.
*   **Automatic Optimization:** We've added a system that **instantly shrinks large images** when you upload them.
    *   *Result:* Your pages load faster, users stay longer, and your upload time is reduced from minutes to seconds.
*   **Card vs. Hero Images:** You can now choose a specific "Thumbnail" image for the project list and a different "Hero" image for the detail page.
